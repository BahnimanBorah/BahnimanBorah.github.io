The Idea:

The idea behind this project is simple and explanatory enough. In today's world we have happened to observe many natural and human caused catastrophies. Forest wildfires, demolishing buildings and even wars. Buildings and forests caught up by fire are some of the most common things we have observed up untill now.

Voice Controlled High Speed Autonomous Drone

is a drone that is provided with the abilities of helping the rescue teams in such catastrophic scenarios. The drone is equipped with sensors able to detect obstacles, fire, humans and directions. The drone will have the ability to follow commands that are programmed to perform specialized task like examining the building to detect that which part of the building has the highest heat comming out of it. This will eventually help the fire team to identify and focus on the core coordinates of the fire generated.
